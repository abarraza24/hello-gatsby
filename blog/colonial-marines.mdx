When you're dropping into a bug hunt on a hostile world, you don't go in empty-handed. The United States Colonial Marines (USCM) rely on the best military hardware Weyland-Yutani's military division can manufacture.

If you want to survive an encounter with a Xenomorph swarm, you *need* to know your gear.

### Standard Issue Loadout

* **M41A Pulse Rifle:** The classic. Fires 10mm explosive-tip caseless ammunition, complete with an under-barrel 30mm pump-action grenade launcher.
* **M56 Smartgun:** A heavy, heavy weapon attached to a tracking harness. It practically aims itself, tracking targets via infrared sensors.
* **UA 571-C Remote Sentry Gun:** Place these in a corridor, set the automated trigger, and watch them chew through targets at 1100 rounds per minute.
* **The Motion Tracker:** That slow, rhythmic *beep*... *beep*... *beep* that speeds up as they get closer. It doesn't tell you *what* is coming, only that it's fast and it's **under 10 meters away**.
